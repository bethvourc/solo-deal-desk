# Discovery call transcript — BrightCart analytics dashboard

**Date:** 2026-06-18  
**Present:** Morgan Patel (Marketing Manager, BrightCart) · Riley Chen (founder, Northstar Ops)

---

**Riley:** What made you reach out?

**Morgan:** We might need some kind of analytics dashboard. Leadership keeps asking for
more reporting, and I am looking around to see what options exist.

**Riley:** What is broken in the current process?

**Morgan:** I would not say broken. We have spreadsheets. They are annoying, but they work
well enough for now. It would just be nice to have something prettier.

**Riley:** Is there a specific deadline or business event driving this?

**Morgan:** Not really. Maybe later this year. I mostly wanted to get a sense of pricing.

**Riley:** Who would approve a project like this?

**Morgan:** I would need to bring it to our VP. I do not know if she wants to spend on it.

**Riley:** Do you have a budget range?

**Morgan:** No budget yet. We are trying not to spend much this quarter.

**Riley:** What would success look like?

**Morgan:** Hard to say. A dashboard people like using, I guess. We have not scoped the
metrics or data sources yet.

**Riley:** Are you evaluating other options?

**Morgan:** Yes, I am talking to a few dashboard vendors and maybe someone internal can do
it if they have time.
